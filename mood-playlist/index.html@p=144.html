About

MOOD Playlist is a playlist generator that allows you to automatically create personalised Spotify playlists that suit your mood and taste. Creating a playlist takes just a few short steps, and and the result is instantly saved right into your Spotify account.

Every single MOOD Playlist is unique, and uses your mood and personal taste to hand-pick the tracks, so you can be certain that no matter what mood you’re in, your perfect playlist can be found right here.

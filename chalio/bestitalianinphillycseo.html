Enjoy Quality Cuisine with These Best Italian Restaurants in Philly

see the Best of City Walker

There is simply nothing quite like authentic Italian cuisine. A mix of rich, savory pastas, sophisticated bruschetta, and of course fine wines. Whether you are visiting Philly for the first time or have been here before, chances are you wouldn’t mind trying some impeccable food. Read on for some helpful suggestions about some of the very best Italian restaurants in Philly.

Vetri Ristorante

At 1312 Spruce Street, nestled in a red brick building, you will find Vetri Ristorante where some of the finest Italian food is served. Executive Chef Marc Vetri presents each guest with a tantalizing six to eight course menu. This location is perfect for someone who is looking for an upscale and fine dining experience. The formal ambience casts a romantic mood and the cuisine is truly one-of-a-kind. With an incredible host of the freshest pasta dishes, you are bound to find something that you will remember.

Modo Mio

Modo Mio is a prime destination for private events, rehearsal dinners, and the finest Italian cuisine around. With moderate pricing and a convenient location at 161 W Girard Avenue, you can enjoy delicious dishes from bruschetta to homemade lasagna. Because dining at Modo Mio is so highly anticipated, reservations are required so be sure to plan ahead if you choose to visit this location from this list of best Italian restaurants in Philly.

Tony Luke’s

A cheaper alternative and one of the best new Italian restaurants in Philadelphia is Tony Luke’s. This modest sandwich shop is known for serving some of the most sumptuous sliders around. Using only meats that are locally sourced and free of antibiotics and hormones, means each sandwich is fashioned to perfection using only the highest quality of ingredients. Their menu boasts a huge selection of sandwiches including specialty sliders, chicken cutlets, hoagies, veal cutlets, roast pork, and so much more. If you are looking for an authentic Italian sandwich and a casual setting, then Tony Luke’s is the perfect fit for you.

Giorgio on Pine

Established at 1328 Pine Street, Giorgio on Pine is another affordable option if you want to try one of the best Italian restaurants in Philly. This location is especially convenient if you wish to order online and enjoy a takeout meal in the comfort of your hotel or home. With classics like gnocchi and beef ragout, you can enjoy a delicious Italian meal for a competitive price.

There is no better time than now to choose one of the best Italian restaurants in Philly to try. Whether you decide to enjoy a formal dinner at Vetri Ristorante or Modo Mio or would rather stay casual with Tony Luke’s or Giorgio on Pine, you can’t go wrong either way.
